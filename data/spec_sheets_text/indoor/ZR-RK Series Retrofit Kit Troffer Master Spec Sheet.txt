Product Description
The ZRRK™ LED Retrofit Troffer Kit provides energy productivity and code compliance – all with 
installation that's so intuitive and simple. The ZRRK Series delivers from 2,030 to 8,300 lumens and 
85 CRI quality light and is perfect for both retrofit and renovation. Available with 0-10V dimming or  
SmartCast Technology, which incorporates integrated ambient and occupancy sensing and wireless 
communication resulting in lower electricity bills, reduced maintenance and an improved total cost of 
ownership over traditional lighting control systems. The ZRRK™ LED Retrofit Troffer Kit installs into most 
existing fluorescent troffers, with a 3 part doorkit design that snaps and locks into place for quick and 
easy upgrading.
