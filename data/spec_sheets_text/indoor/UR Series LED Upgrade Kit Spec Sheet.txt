Product Description
The UR Series LED upgrade kit provides up to 8,400 Lumens of 80 CRI light while achieving up to 135 
lumens per watt at the system level. This innovate kit is designed for retrofitting exisiting 1' x 2' (305mm 
x 610mm), 2' x 2' (610mm x 610mm), 1' x 4' (305mm x 1219mm) & 2' x 4' (610mm x 1219mm) fixtures 
to energy saving LED technology. The UR series is available in multiple color temperatures and offers 
0-10V smooth dimming. The UR series upgrade is easy to install and fits into almost any existing 
linear floursescent fixture making it a perfect upgrade option where energy savings and long life are 
important.
